The Friends of Ryebank Fields celebrated the August Bank Holiday weekend by getting together for a community picnic.

The sun shone on residents of all ages, and some extremely handsome dogs, as they met friends old and new and enjoyed the wild green space. We gathered lots of signatures for our petition and even raised some money for the printing of our fabulous posters.

Head to the Friends of Ryebank Fields Facebook and Twitter to stay up to date with how this much loved green space is used by the local community and wildlife.
